Nepal is mostly only known due to its association with the tallest mountain in the world, Mt. Everest, so here's a bit more on Nepal beyond that to those who are curious. Nepal is the mixing pot of southeast asia and its culture. As the country which has India to one side and Tibet/ China to the other, it creates for a very diverse and versatile exchange of cultures and traditions. Although Nepal has a huge population percentage of hinduism other religions like buddhism, christianity, muslim aren't uncommon. Similarly, although Nepali is the official national language, Nepal also has over 120 major languages which are recognized as national languages. Due to these Nepal is a very welcoming country with diverse cultures, foods, religions, clothes, etc... which allows for another worldly experience for outsiders when they visit.
